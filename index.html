Hi, I'm BAO Yang (鲍杨).

I am an Assistant Professor of Information Systems in the Antai College of Economics and Management at the Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China. Before joining SJTU, I have worked as Research Fellow in the Division of Accounting of the Nanyang Business School at the Nanyang Technological University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Information Systems from the National University of Singapore, and bachelor & master degree in Management from Nanjing University.
